Mediterranean Quinoa Salad

Mediterranean Quinoa Salad is a culinary masterpiece that transports your taste buds to the sun-kissed shores of the Mediterranean. Bursting with vibrant colors and flavors, this salad is a celebration of wholesome ingredients and a testament to the region's rich culinary heritage.
At its heart lies quinoa, a nutrient-packed grain, adorned with cherry tomatoes, crisp cucumbers, Kalamata olives, crumbled feta cheese, and finely chopped red onions. These ingredients form a harmonious blend that's as visually appealing as it is delicious.
A zesty dressing, made with fresh lemon juice and olive oil, ties it all together, infusing the salad with a refreshing tang. Each bite is a journey through the Mediterranean, where the simplicity of ingredients shines through, and health and flavor coalesce effortlessly.

Ingredients :-

  • 1 cup quinoa, cooked and cooled
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1 cucumber, diced
  • 1/2 cup Kalamata olives, pitted and sliced
  • 1/2 cup crumbled feta cheese
  • 1/4 cup red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions :-

  1. In a large bowl, combine cooked quinoa, cherry tomatoes, cucumber, olives, feta cheese, and red onion.
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together lemon juice, olive oil, salt, and pepper.
  3. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine.
  4. Chill in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes before serving.
