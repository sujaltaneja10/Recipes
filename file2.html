Chicken Alfredo

Indulge in Creamy Delight: Chicken Alfredo

Chicken Alfredo is a beloved Italian-American dish that has earned its place as a comfort food classic. This delectable creation combines tender strips of chicken with fettuccine pasta, all generously coated in a luxuriously creamy Alfredo sauce. The star of this show is the velvety Alfredo sauce, a blend of heavy cream, butter, and Parmesan cheese, simmered to perfection. Garlic adds a fragrant, aromatic note, elevating the dish's depth of flavor.
Chicken Alfredo is a culinary symphony, a harmony of textures and tastes. The tender chicken contrasts beautifully with the silky pasta, while the sauce envelops each bite in rich, savory goodness. This dish is versatile, making it perfect for weeknight dinners or special occasions. \

Ingredients :-

  • 8 oz (225g) fettuccine pasta
  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions :-

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to package instructions.
  2. Season chicken breasts with salt and pepper, then cook in a skillet until no longer pink.
  3. Slice into strips.
  4. In the same skillet, sauté minced garlic until fragrant.
  5. Add heavy cream and Parmesan cheese. Stir until the sauce thickens.
  6. Toss cooked pasta and sliced chicken in the sauce. Serve hot.
